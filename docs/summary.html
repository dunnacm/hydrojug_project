Key assumptions implemented

  • Combined demand: forecast plus open orders, but to avoid double‑counting we treat open orders as replacing forecast when open orders exceed forecast (per SKU per channel).
  • Scope: only UPCs that appear in the Inventory sheet are assessed.
  • Supply: starting On Hand plus Incoming that has an expected date inside the horizon.
  • Lead time: 90 days (used for interpretation/recommendations; not explicitly optimized in this example output).
  • Data quality: inventory rows with an incoming date of 2025‑03‑17 were treated as a likely year typo and shifted to 2026‑03‑17; rows with ######## as a date were treated as unknown.
